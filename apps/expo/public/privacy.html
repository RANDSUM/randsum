RANDSUM Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

RANDSUM ("we", "us", "our") provides a dice notation playground application available on iOS, Android, and web. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The RANDSUM app:

  • Does not require account creation or sign-in
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Does not use advertising identifiers

Local Storage

The app stores your theme preference (light or dark mode) locally on your device using standard platform storage (AsyncStorage on mobile, localStorage on web). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

URL Parameters (Web Only)

On the web version at randsum.io, the dice notation you enter may be reflected in the URL as a query parameter (e.g., ?n=4d6L) to enable link sharing. This is entirely client-side and no server records these URLs.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry services. We do not use cookies. We do not use fingerprinting.

Third-Party Services

The app is distributed through:

  • Apple App Store (iOS) — subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store (Android) — subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • EAS Hosting (web) — hosted by Expo/EAS, subject to Expo's Privacy Policy

These platforms may collect their own usage data as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not control or have access to any data collected by these platforms.

Children's Privacy

The RANDSUM app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, there is no children's data to protect. The app is a dice rolling utility suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at:
