## Introduction

`@randsum/games` is a handy collection of utilties for specific games.

- the `SalvageUnion` module gives contextual results based on the table you are rolling against.
- The `Root` module asks for your bonus and gives you the success level of the roll.
- the `FifthEdition` module rolls D20's with the best of them, with built-in advantage support.
- the `Blades` module gives you the perfect utility for all your Forged in the dark games.
